Design Language & Principles
Warm Minimalism

The site avoids cool grays and blues entirely. All surfaces, texts and borders are warm-tinted. This creates a human, approachable premium feel — clinical warmth.

Editorial Rhythm

Large serif headings contrasted with light-weight sans-serif body text. Multi-line headline splits ("Our process / is really simple.") create visual cadence.

Confident Typography

H1 is 56–64px. Generous white space. Italic is used selectively in serif for emotional resonance. No decorative icons — text is the design element.

Social Proof Density

Client logos in horizontal scrolling ticker. 6 testimonials in carousel. Pricing backed by satisfaction guarantee. Trust is architected systematically.
